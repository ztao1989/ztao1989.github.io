Brief Bio

I am a final-year PhD student in the ECE Department at Northeastern University. I have broad research interests in machine learning, data mining and computer vision. Particularly, I focus on learning feature representations with reconstruction and applying it in data cluster analysis, user modeling, and visual understanding task. I am on the academic job market now.

I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yun Raymond Fu, and work with many fantastic researchers in the SMILE Lab.
