Brief Bio

I am a fourth-year PhD student working in SMILE Lab at Northeastern University. My advisor is Prof. Yun Fu. I have broad reserach interests in machine learning, data mining and computer vision. Particularly, I foucus on data cluster analysis, visual understaing, and user modeling through learning feature representations in a data reconstruction way.
