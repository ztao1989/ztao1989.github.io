Brief Bio

I am currently a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Santa Clara University. I received my Ph.D. degree from the ECE Department at Northeastern University, under the supervision of Prof. Yun Raymond Fu. Before that, I obtained my B.E. and M.E. degrees from Tianjin University in 2012 and 2015, respectively.

I am looking for highly-motivated graduate students to work with me. Please drop me an email if interested.
